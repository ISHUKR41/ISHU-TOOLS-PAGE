# Skill: podcast-marketing

## Purpose
Creates comprehensive podcast marketing strategies — guest outreach templates, social media promotion plans, audiogram concepts, cross-promotion strategies, listener growth tactics, Spotify/Apple Podcasts optimization, and email newsletter integration for podcast promotion.

## When to Use
- Podcast is launched and needs listeners
- Need to promote a new episode across platforms
- Need guest outreach emails for interview shows
- Need social media clips/quotes from episodes
- Need to write Apple Podcasts / Spotify descriptions
- Need a listener growth strategy

## Core Marketing Channels for Podcasts

### Organic Channels
| Channel | Content Type | Frequency |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Instagram | Audiogram clips, quote cards | 3x/week |
| LinkedIn | Key insights, episode text posts | 2x/week |
| Twitter/X | Episode threads, live-tweeting | Daily |
| YouTube | Full episode video, clips, shorts | Per episode |
| WhatsApp | Status, community groups | Weekly |
| Email Newsletter | Episode summary + links | Weekly |
| Reddit | Related subreddit participation | 2x/week |

### Platform Optimization

#### Apple Podcasts Optimization
```
Title: [Show Name] | [Primary Keyword]
Subtitle: [Secondary keyword-rich tagline]
Description: First 250 characters are above the fold.
Lead with: "Join [Host] as they [what listeners get]..."
Include keywords: naturally in first 2 paragraphs
Categories: Choose 2 most relevant subcategories
Artwork: 3000×3000px, legible at 55×55px thumbnail

Episode titles format: "[Number]: [Benefit-driven title with keywords]"
Example: "47: How to Compress PDF Files for Free (Save 80% Space)"
```

#### Spotify Optimization  
```
Show description: 600 words max (use full limit)
Episode description: Include key topics, guests, timestamps
Tags: Use all 5 tag slots with long-tail keywords
Categories: Match Apple Podcasts categories
Episode titles: Same as Apple (they syndicate)
```

## Guest Outreach Templates

### Cold Outreach Email (to Potential Guest)
```
Subject: Podcast Guest Invitation — [Show Name] (Quick invite)

Hi [Guest First Name],

I've been following your work on [specific thing] — particularly 
your [article/tweet/talk] about [specific topic]. Really valuable stuff.

I host "[Show Name]," a podcast about [brief description] with 
[audience size/description]. Our listeners are [who they are].

I'd love to have you on for a 30-minute conversation about 
[specific angle relevant to guest's expertise]. No prep needed — 
just a casual conversation that I think our listeners would love.

Past guests include: [2-3 notable names if applicable]
Episode examples: [link to similar episode]

Would you be open to it? Totally understand if you're swamped.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Podcast name + link]
[Episode count + "downloads" or social proof]
```

### Follow-up Template (After 1 Week)
```
Subject: Re: Podcast Guest Invitation — quick follow-up

Hi [Name],

Just checking in on my note from last week. Totally fine if timing 
doesn't work — no pressure at all.

If you are interested, I've kept [two date options] open on my calendar.
The interview is usually 30 min, recorded over Zoom.

Thanks either way for the work you do on [their topic]!

[Name]
```

## Listener Growth Tactics

### Quick Wins (Month 1)
```
1. WARM AUDIENCE FIRST
   - Ask friends, family, classmates to subscribe + review
   - Share in WhatsApp groups, Telegram channels you're in
   - Post in college Discord/Facebook groups

2. REDDIT STRATEGY
   - Find 3-5 subreddits related to your topic
   - Participate genuinely (don't just drop links)
   - After 10+ karma in the sub, share relevant episodes

3. COLLABORATE WITH OTHER SMALL PODCASTERS
   - Guest-swap: appear on their show, they appear on yours
   - Share each other's episodes to cross-audiences

4. LEVERAGE EVERY PIECE OF CONTENT
   - 1 episode → 1 blog post + 3 Instagram posts + 5 tweets + 1 newsletter
```

### Growth Strategy (Month 2-6)
```
1. APPLE PODCASTS REVIEWS
   - Ask guests to leave a 5-star review
   - Include CTA in every episode: "If you got value, leave a review"
   - Reviews improve discoverability ranking

2. PODCAST DIRECTORIES
   Submit to: Spotify, Apple, Google, Amazon Music, Stitcher, 
   Pocket Casts, Overcast, Podchaser, Listen Notes

3. NEWSLETTER INTEGRATION
   - Add podcast player embed to email
   - Send episode summaries as standalone newsletter

4. YOUTUBE SHORTS
   - Cut 60-second clips from episodes
   - Shorts get 5-10x more reach than long-form
   - Add captions (90% of social video is watched muted)
```
